Delete Your UniConnect Account

Your privacy matters. This page explains how to permanently delete your account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the UniConnect app.
  2. Sign in to your account.
  3. Navigate to Profile → Settings.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Review the information presented.
  6. Confirm your deletion request.

30-Day Recovery Period

When you request account deletion, your account is immediately hidden from other users and enters a 30-day recovery period.

  • Your public profile becomes invisible.
  • Your gallery images are hidden.
  • Your profile cards are removed from discovery.
  • Your matching preferences are disabled.
  • Your verification badges are hidden.
Changed your mind?

Simply sign back into your account within 30 days to restore your account and all associated information.

Permanent Deletion

If you do not log in during the 30-day recovery period, your account and associated data are permanently deleted.


The following data is permanently removed:
  • Email address
  • Phone authentication references
  • User profile information
  • Profile picture
  • Gallery images
  • Matching preferences
  • Verification status
  • Chat messages and user-generated content associated with your account where applicable
  • System references required for account identification
This action is irreversible once the 30-day recovery period has expired.

Need Assistance?

If you're unable to access your account but would still like it deleted, contact the UniConnect Support Team.
